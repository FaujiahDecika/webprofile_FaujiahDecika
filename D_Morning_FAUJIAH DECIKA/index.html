Education

I believe that education is the key to success, and I am committed to continuously inspiring and supporting others on their educational journey. I have a diverse educational background that has shaped me into the individual I am today. Here are some of my educational achievements:

MIS Nurul Islam

Madrasah Ibtidaiyah Nurul Islam Parit Seratus is an Islamic elementary school, equivalent to other elementary schools, situated in Sungai Sebesi, Kundur Subdistrict, Karimun Regency, Riau Islands Province. I attended this school for a period of 6 years. During my time there, I not only focused on regular social studies but also deepened my religious knowledge. I studied subjects such as Arabic language, faith and morality (akidah akhlak), and other Islamic subjects.

SMPN 1 Kundur

SMP Negeri 1 Kundur is located at Jl. Jendral Sudirman No.051 Tanjung Batu Kundur, TANJUNG BATU KOTA, Kundur District, Karimun Regency, Riau Islands. I completed my three-year junior high school education there. This school is known for its commitment to honesty and fairness in grading its students.

SMAN 19 Medan

SMA Negeri 19 Medan, or State Senior High School 19 Medan, is one of the public high schools located in the North Sumatra Province, Indonesia. I attended this school for 3 years and pursued the natural sciences track, graduating in the year 2020. The school's address is situated at Jl. Seruwai No.1, Sei Mati, Medan Labuhan Subdistrict, Medan City, North Sumatra.

Universitas Maritim Raja Ali Haji (UMRAH)

Maritime University of Raja Ali Haji is a public higher education institution located in the city of Tanjungpinang, Kepulauan Riau Province. It was founded on August 1, 2007. I enrolled in the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program in 2021, and I am currently in my fifth semester. The university is located at Jl. Raya Dompak, Dompak, Bukit Bestari District, Tanjung Pinang City, Kepulauan Riau.
